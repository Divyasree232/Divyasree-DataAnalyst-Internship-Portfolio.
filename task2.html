Objective

Uncover patterns and relationships in the data using EDA and SQL, then propose a dashboard mock-up for business reporting.

Key Steps

  • Descriptive statistics and univariate analysis for numerical and categorical data.
  • Create initial visualizations such as histograms and bar charts.
  • Answer business questions with SQL queries covering filtering, aggregation, and joins.
  • Multivariate analysis using scatter plots, heatmaps, and pair plots.
  • Design a static dashboard mock-up with top KPIs.

Deliverables

  • EDA report
  • SQL queries with results
  • Dashboard mock-up concept

Timeline: 12 days
